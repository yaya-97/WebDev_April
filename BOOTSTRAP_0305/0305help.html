We hope you'll find what you're looking for.

Our goal is to make searching for travel as transparent as possible. That’s why prices on FirmamentFinder always include an estimate of all taxes and charges, including any agency fees charged by online travel agencies.

As a travel search engine, we don’t advertise prices ourselves, and instead we display them on behalf of the travel providers (airlines and travel agents). FirmamentFinder doesn’t influence prices at all, either, and we are only able to display the rates and availability that the travel providers pass through to us to be displayed.

When we transfer you to the airline or travel agent website to complete your booking, all flight details including prices will be updated and displayed. The final price you pay for your tickets may change because of additional options offered by the provider, such as luggage, seating options or travel insurance (although these should always be optional).
You should get a booking confirmation by email from the company you bought your travel from after completing payment for your booking. This can sometimes go into your spam/junk mail folder so it’s always worth checking there.

There are 1000s of travel agencies, airlines, hotels and car hire companies that you can buy from through our site and app. We call them “partners”. If you were redirected to a partner’s site, or you bought from a partner within our site or app, then you’ll need to contact that partner directly about this.
